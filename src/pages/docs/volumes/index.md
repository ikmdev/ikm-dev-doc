### Volume 6: ANF Ballot

<br></br>
Volume 6 highlights the importance of using a standardized representation of clinical statements, like the Analysis Normal Form (ANF), to ensure they are uniformly understood, reproducible, and useful across and within healthcare systems. <br></br>
Volume 10 highlights a critical component of healthcare, ensuring the confidentiality and integrity of patients' personal health information (PHI) while simultaneously supporting their ability to access their own data. Although IKM, and other entities like SNOMED CTÂ® and LOINCÂ®, do not directly handle patient data, they play a critical role in data privacy through standardized data management practices.

<a href="/Volume-16.pdf" target="_blank" class={styles.pdfLink}> PDF Version ğŸ“ </a>

<br></br>
Volume 16 covers temporal relationships in healthcare, which describe how events are related over time.
